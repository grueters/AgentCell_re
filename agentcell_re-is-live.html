AgentCell_re is live!

Uwe Grueters

Uwe Grueters and Leon Metzger proudly present AgentCell_re (2021), a revitalization of the ancient Inidividual-based Chemotaxis Model AgentCell (2007) in RePast Simphony 2.8. As its predecessor AgentCell_re simulates movement of E. coli bacteria along an aspartate gradient using stochsim to resemble the metabolic receptor network.
