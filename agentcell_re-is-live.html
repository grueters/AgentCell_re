AgentCell_re is live!

Uwe Grueters February 18, 2021

Uwe Grueters and Leon Metzger proudly present AgentCell_re (2021), a revitalization of the ancient Inidividual-based Chemotaxis Model AgentCell (2007) in RePast Simphony 2.8. As its predecessor AgentCell_re simulates movement of E. coli bacteria along an aspartate gradient using stochsim to resemble the metabolic receptor network. The Java/C++ code can be found at:  https://github.com/grueters/AgentCell_re .

Do you need help with installing or running AgentCell_re or do you have further questions? We are more than happy to support you. Just write a message to:
